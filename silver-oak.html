Project Overview

  • Role: Front-End Developer
  • Duration: 3 weeks
  • Client: Silver Oak Contracting
  • Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Type: Client website template

Silver Oak Contracting approached me to design and develop a professional website template for their structural steel fabrication and erection business. The goal was to create a modern, trustworthy online presence that showcases their services and projects.

I developed a responsive single-page website featuring a video hero section, service breakdowns, project portfolio, and contact form. The design emphasizes industrial strength and professionalism while maintaining clean, accessible UX.

The template was delivered as a complete, production-ready codebase that the client could deploy and customize as needed.
