Mobile App Design Project: Snack Delivery Service

Project Overview

This project focused on designing a mobile app experience for a healthy snack subscription platform. The app allows users to discover, shop, and subscribe to clean, diet-friendly snacks. My goal was to create a modern, intuitive interface that supports a seamless shopping journey—from onboarding to checking out.

Project Goals

  • Streamline snack discovery with intuitive navigation and filtering
  • Design for personalization and diet-specific browsing
  • Make autoship (subscription ordering) easy and flexible
  • Create a visual system that feels clean, trustworthy, and vibrant

Task List & User Flow

I identified essential user tasks and mapped a journey flow diagram to plan the architecture and key interactions.

Main Tasks:

  • Create/edit an account
  • Take onboarding quiz and set preferences
  • Browse by diet, brand, or category
  • View product details and nutritional info
  • Add to cart, favorites, or autoship
  • Place orders and track deliveries
  • Manage autoship frequency and quantity
  • Contact support and read FAQs

Flow Highlights: Users can access curated snack suggestions after completing onboarding. They can then browse or filter based on dietary needs (e.g., gluten-free, keto), add items to cart or subscription, and easily track upcoming orders.

Visual Design & Branding

I designed a modular, clean UI system to support both product exploration and purchase flow:

  • Built a full color system with green, red, yellow, and neutral tones to create hierarchy and visual balance
  • Designed and tested component variants for buttons, cards, icons, and onboarding steps
  • Ensured accessible contrast and reusable style tokens for scalable design

Key Screens

  • Opening & Onboarding: Clean welcome screen, quiz-based preference setup
  • Home Screen: Personalized recommendations, featured brands, and snack categories
  • Browse Screens: Tabs to explore by diet, brand, category, and trending items
  • Product Page: Focus on nutrition info, reviews, pricing, and quick add-to-cart
  • Cart & Autoship: Simple flow for editing, scheduling, and managing recurring deliveries

Component System

I developed a flexible design system that included:

  • Button states (normal, hover, active)
  • Input fields with validation
  • Chip filters and icons
  • Reusable cards for snacks, brands, and categories
  • Visual feedback for onboarding progress, cart updates, and selection states

Reflection

This project pushed me to balance information density with usability, especially when designing for health-conscious shoppers with specific dietary needs. I learned a lot about building scalable design systems, user onboarding, and simplifying recurring purchase flows. It's one of my favorite pieces because of the thoughtful flow and clean design I was able to achieve.
